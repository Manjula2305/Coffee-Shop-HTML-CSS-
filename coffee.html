About Karupatti Coffee

Karupatti coffee is a popular South Indian beverage made with coffee and palm jaggery, a sweetener derived from the sap of palm trees. Karupatti coffee is a traditional South Indian drink that combines coffee with Karupatti in Tamil.

Karupatti Coffee, a cherished traditional beverage from South India, is much more than just a drink—it’s a wholesome experience rooted in history, health, and the art of simple living. This delightful coffee gets its unique flavor and sweetness from karupatti, also known as palm jaggery, a natural sweetener made from the sap of palm trees.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver an exceptional coffee experience that is both authentic and health-conscious. By choosing Karupatti Coffee, you’re embracing a lifestyle that values traditional flavors, natural ingredients, and eco-friendly practices.

Our Vision

To become a global ambassador for traditional South Indian beverages, inspiring people around the world to reconnect with the flavors of heritage and the goodness of nature.

The Health Advantage

Karupatti Coffee is not just about great taste—it’s a journey to wellness. Palm jaggery, a key ingredient, is known for its numerous health benefits:

  • Boosts energy naturally.
  • Improves digestion.
  • Supports weight management.
  • Promotes overall wellness with its rich nutritional profile.
